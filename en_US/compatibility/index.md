#  Logiciel

> **IMPORTANT**
>
> Jeedom only supports Debian Stretch (not recommended), Buster (end of life) and Bullseye (recommended). Any other version (Wheezy, Jessie…) or distribution (Ubuntu…) is not supported by Jeedom.
